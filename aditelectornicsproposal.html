Executive Summary

This proposal outlines a 3-phase roadmap to create a unified digital ecosystem for Adit Compute World. The objective is to combine eCommerce and service automation to reduce operational overload, set up for scaling operations, and deliver customer-first experiences.

Phase 1

Shopify Store

A modern, electronics-focused eCommerce platform.

Phase 2

Service Automation

A custom web-app for streamlined after-sales support.

Phase 3

Mobile App

Dedicated iOS & Android apps for enhanced engagement.

Our Commitment to Partnership

We believe in building long-term relationships. This proposal includes our commitment to provide continued support and handle small developments for up to 18 months post-completion of the final delivered phase. This ensures your digital ecosystem runs smoothly and evolves with your business needs.

Phase 2: Service Query Automation

A Custom WebApp for After-Sales Support

We will develop a full-stack web application from scratch. The customer-facing form and admin dashboard will be built with React/Next.js for a modern UI. The backend, built on Node.js, will handle the business logic for ticket assignment and integrate with the WhatsApp Business API for real-time notifications to engineers.
